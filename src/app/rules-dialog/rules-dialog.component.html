Quiz Rules

  • Answers can be up to 2 decimal places. Rounding for the 2nd decimal position follows normal rounding rules [>= 5, round up].
  • If you answer three questions incorrectly, the quiz will automatically end and save your current score. They do not need to be in a row.
  • If you skip a question, you will lose one point.
  • If you answer a question incorrectly, you do not lose a point. You just do not gain one.
  • If you answer a question correctly, you gain one point.
